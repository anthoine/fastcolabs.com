An email list is one of the most powerful tools you have to keep in touch with the people who use your app. Every day, Dave Pell's emails reach 30,000 addicted subscribers, delivering what he thinks is the day's most interesting news and averaging an open rate of 50 percent. The NextDraft app has been downloaded over 75,000 times on iPhone and iPad. "I want to be aware of important stories, know about well-written features, and waste a few minutes watching a guy solve a Rubik's cube while juggling," says Pell.
